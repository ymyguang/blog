逻辑词代换

表示因果

  1. 表示原因

    • because=> in that;
    • bacause of=due to = be responsible for

  2. 表示结果：

    • so = therefore = as a resule/consequence

表示对比

  1. 相似性的对比：

    • likewise = similarly = equally = at the same time(同样地)

  2. 差异性对比：

    • but = by contrast = alternatively

表示递进

beside = also = in addition = moreover = in other word ，另外
